## Quickstart

Here you can swap and rapidly launch Bitcoin Runes using our new Perfect Fair Launch mechanism. 

Perfect Fair Launches serve the purpose to fully block soft rugs _(dev selling, insiders/snipers/bundlers selling)_ — and offer many other investor protections. 

>**Sophisticated token launch scams result in an estimated $5B+ of losses per year mostly through platforms like Pump Fun. We solve this.**

## Using The Platform

Every token that bonds on Meme Dealer will instantly get a seed funded DAO for the community to earn, in addition to staking, vesting and a seeded LP within the platform. All of this on day one, for every bonded coin. 

These elements come together to create a **Perfect Fair Launch.**
